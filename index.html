Dr. Xiaohan Chen is a postdoctoral research fellow in the KIOS Research and Innovation Center of Excellence at the University of Cyprus, supervised by Prof. Marios Polycarpou. He obtained his Ph.D. degree in the Department of Electrical Eng and Electronics at the University of Liverpool in 2024, supervised by Prof. Rui Yang.

Research interests: Transfer Learning, Graph Neural Networks, Few-Shot Learning, Temporal Signal Analysis, Fault Diagnosis.
